JS Animal 3D

Once we had decided to architect the delivery of 3d anatomy content via a combination of utilizing SketchFab as a 3d content hoster paired with an elegant JavaScript powered website, I went straight to work on creating mocks via Balsamiq3. My first step in this creative process involved researching similar products currently in the market over a variety of devices. After synthesizing a variety of approaches, I concluded a simple and intuitive user interface that emphasizes the 3d content was not only the right fit for our users' primary use-case, but also a practical architecture decision that would launch us to a minimum viable product sooner.

We are currently in an early start-up formation phase and are working on building an elegant platform as well as continuing to develop 3d content. Our mission is to be the provider of high-fidelity 3d animal anatomy in order to enhance the learning experience for vet students, teachers, and professionals.
