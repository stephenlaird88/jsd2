Dash Nexus

DashNexus is a next-generation decentralized governance platform designed to support the needs of the rapidly growing Dash DAO. Our goal is to dramatically improve the governance experience for both MNOs and Proposal Owners by resolving problems and reducing friction in the governance process. More specifically, we want to provide Proposal Owners with additional tools to present their offerings to the network, while streamlining the proposal evaluation process for MNOs and reducing the workload involved in making well informed decisions while also providing additional tools which make running a masternode just a little easier.

Mithril Vault

The Mithril Vault platform is designed for users in the Mithril Ecosystem to be able to withdraw or shift the Mithril cryptocurrency (top 100 cryptocurrency) acquired through social mining. In our work with the Mithril team, we designed a gamification staking mechanism in order to encourage users of the Lit social media app to stake their Mithril tokens to supercharge the Mithril they can acquire through social mining. Social mining is a mechanism that essentially rewards users of the Lit app with Mithril tokens when they post stories that receive views, likes, and watch time. Through this design, we were able to meet the business goal of increasing ecosystem value while at the same time increasing user engagement as this further fueled users to post more quality posts.

SF Decentralized Governance Meetup

The San Francisco Decentralized Governance Meetup is a forum for all members participating in the blockchain space (developers, entrepreneurs, non-profit orgs, legal experts, and enthusiasts) to learn about the latest use-cases utilizing blockchain technology and how these projects are applying governance. While we live in an exciting time period where technical breakthroughs in blockchain technology enable us to experiment with many governing systems at a rapid pace, we need to also in parallel apply the best governance and cultural practices that history and other disciplines have to offer. We host speakers from a variety of disciplines to foster debate and to uncover key lessons that the community can apply in their own blockchain projects. We welcome proposals for talks at stephen@blockstate.co. We are specifically interested in the following topics:


1) Blockchain projects that are thinking about or already have a decentralized governance model in place for activities such as maintaining the protocol or voting on new proposal features/issues. 2) Academia or any other organization who are at the forefront of understanding the pros/cons of varying blockchain governance models: Futarchy, DAO, Liquid Democracy, Quadratic Voting, etc. 3) Experts in the open-source technical standards community with strong opinions on topics such as on-chain vs off-chain voting (or no voting). 4) Experts tackling regulatory and compliance issues so that blockchain projects can be responsible and accountable with managing funds raised.

Animal 3D Anatomy

Once we had decided to architect the delivery of 3d anatomy utilizing webgl browser technology, I went straight to work on creating mocks via Balsamiq3. My first step in this creative process involved researching similar products currently in the market over a variety of devices. After synthesizings several approaches, I concluded a simple and intuitive user interface that emphasizes the 3d content was not only the right fit for our users' primary use-case, but also a practical architecture decision that would launch us to a minimum viable product sooner. Actually building this platform ended up being quite challenging and a great learning experience for our team of 3d modelers and UX designers. The build involves creating 3d models through 3d software such as Z-brush and Auto-cad. To render this content to the web we use Three.js as our javascript webgl renderer, Django as our back-end controller, AWS S3 as our static files server, and Heroku as our main server.

Our mission is to be the provider of high-fidelity 3d animal anatomy in order to enhance the learning experience for vet students, teachers, and professionals.
